MOVIE REVIEW

Movie Name:YATRA

Review :

Yatra is a gripping and engaging emotional ride. The movie showcases YSR’s historical Yatra in realistic manner. But it runs on slow pace. Hence despite some good moments here and there, the film doesn’t glue us to the screens throughout this movie. However, it may work for YSR’s fans as it gives glimpse of leader’s struggles and his achievement.
